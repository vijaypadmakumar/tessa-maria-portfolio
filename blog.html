Finding Meaning in Marketing

June 10, 2025 · Brand Strategy Marketing

Marketing, to me, isn’t about selling — it’s about storytelling. It’s about connecting with people through emotions, not just visuals and metrics. As someone who’s worked closely with purpose-driven brands, I’ve learned that authenticity always cuts through the noise. When you prioritise the audience’s lived experiences, creative decisions become clearer and more impactful.

The Power of Listening

May 28, 2025 · Communication Growth

Listening is an underrated skill in every field — whether you’re leading a team, developing a brand, or just engaging with a customer. It’s what helps bridge gaps and build relationships that last. A quick check-in, a note of appreciation, or actually acting on feedback can change how people feel about a brand overnight.
